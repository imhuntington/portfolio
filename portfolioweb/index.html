About Ian Huntington

I'm an emerging UX researcher with a background in psychology and behavioral research, focused on understanding how people interact with online platforms and digital tools. My interest in research began at the University of Oregon, where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Psychology and worked in a human factors aviation safety lab analyzing incident data and studying how people interact with complex systems.

Those experiences sparked my interest in how research can uncover patterns in human behavior and translate them into meaningful insights. Since then, I've become particularly interested in how consumers interact with AI-driven products and how factors like trust, control, and transparency influence those experiences.

While my background is rooted in research, I've expanded more into UX and interface design, using tools like Figma to better connect research insights with practical design decisions.

I'm originally from Salem, Oregon and graduated high school in 2020. Outside of research and design, I love spending time outdoors, am a (very intense) Chicago sports fan, and enjoy spending time with friends and family.
